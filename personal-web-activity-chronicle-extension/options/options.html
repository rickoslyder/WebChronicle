About Web Chronicle

Web Chronicle is a personal web activity tracking system that helps you understand your browsing habits while keeping your data private.

Features

  • 📊 Track time spent on websites
  • 📜 Monitor scroll depth and engagement
  • 🔍 AI-powered content summarization
  • 🔎 Semantic search across your history
  • 🔒 Privacy-first: your data stays yours
  • ☁️ Offline support with automatic sync

Privacy

Your browsing data is sent only to your own Cloudflare Worker. No third-party tracking or analytics.
